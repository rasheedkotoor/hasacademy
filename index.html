About Us

HAS (Hazrath Abubakar Siddique (RA) Memorial Education and Charitable Trust) is a trust established in the name of the great Aboobacker Siddique (RA) with the vision of making a positive contribution in the field of education. The main objective of this group is to be a driving force for educational advancement among women community. The trust's projects include HAS Qur'an Academy for boys, HAS She Academy for girls and HAS E-Academy for online studies. The mission of this association is to provide financial assistance in the field of education to the economically backward Students, To host progressive attitude for the revival of the society, to provide shelter to the homeless and to work for the betterment of the nation. The flesh and blood of the group will be the support of well-wishers and dedication of people working in religious service.
